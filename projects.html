Neuro-Symbolic NLP

GRIPS: Grounded Pre-SQL Signature for Structured Data Reasoning

Published at ACL 2026. Language models fail at structured-data reasoning not because they cannot produce SQL, but because they misalign questions with the schema before SQL generation begins. GRIPS is a neurosymbolic framework that separates question-to-schema grounding from SQL generation, improving accuracy by an average of 11 REMS points while reducing token usage by 47.8% across two benchmarks.
